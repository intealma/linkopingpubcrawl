What is it?

Linköping's best guided pub crawl along Ågatan. We take you from Stora Torget all the way to O'Learys — each venue offers a drink to participants during the crawl (O'Learys excluded).

Perfect if you're new in town, want to meet new people, or just want a planned evening without hassle.

Of course we offer alcohol free options.

Rules

How it works

01

Each venue offers one drink to participants

A welcome drink is offered at each pub during the crawl. Drinks at O'Learys are paid by guests themselves.

02

Keep the pace

About 45 min per stop. The group moves on together — no one is left behind.

03

Pre-sale required

Tickets are sold in advance via Swish. No tickets are sold on site — so we know exactly how many we are.

04

20 years & ID

Age limit 20 years. Bring valid ID — required at all stops and entry to O'Learys.
